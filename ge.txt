General Education (General Education)
Program Description

All students pursuing a bachelors degree are required to complete 39 credits of general education coursework
Program Code
GE - BACH
Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs)
Engage in consistent personal evaluation to deepen discipleship of Jesus Christ.
Use a mathematical reasoning process to build arguments supporting a conclusion.
Synthesize viewpoints from multiple sources into a coherent thesis.
Develop personal attributes that promote success during college and throughout life.
Use scientific reasoning to identify truths about the natural world.
Evaluate the personal impact of an artistic or cultural experience.
Apply social science methods of inquiry to analyze social relations.
Program Notes

Breadth Courses: Take 3-6 credits in each area for a total of 15 credits.
